# SidekickAI ‚Äì Easily Embeddable AI Chatbot

**SidekickAI** is a lightweight, easy-to-integrate AI chatbot that you can embed directly into your website. It allows your visitors to interact with a smart AI assistant without the need for complex backend setup or API keys.

---

## Features

- ‚úÖ **Easy Integration:** Copy a single snippet to embed the AI on your site.  
- ‚úÖ **Customizable:** Adjust the look and feel to match your website‚Äôs design.  
- ‚úÖ **Lightweight:** Minimal footprint, no heavy dependencies.  
- ‚úÖ **Responsive:** Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.  
- ‚úÖ **Instant Responses:** Fast, interactive AI chat for your users.
